What is CONNECT? 
CONNECT (Coordinated Network of Networks for Enhancing Collaborative Teams) is an SSHRC-funded partnership that unites four community partners and seven Big Team Social Science (BTSS) networks comprising more than 3,500 researchers from over 70 countries. Our mission is to apply a network-of-networks approach to understand and solve the unique challenges of large-scale social science research, making it more reliable, inclusive, and impactful. Learn more on our website.

Working Group Updates
Metascience
The Metascience team is examining team assessment methodologies from organizational psychology and other relevant fields and is actively developing an instrument to evaluate BTS workflows and team experiences.
Tools & Infrastructure
Currently, the TI working group is generating a data validator, as well as collecting a pre/post survey comparison of collaboration tools for Big Team Science.
Equity, Diversity, & Inclusion
This group has drafted a proposal for a special issue on EDI in Big Team Science. It will be an open call for articles centered around Justice, Equity, Diversity, Inclusion (JEDI) in multisite and international collaborative research.

Partner Updates
The ManyFishes collaboration is thrilled to share that its first paper entitled "ManyFishes: a big team science collaboration on fish comparative cognition" has just been accepted for publication in Animal Cognition. This is a fully open-access review article that is part of a special collection on the diversity of fish cognition.
