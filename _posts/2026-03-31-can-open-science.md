## SAVE THE DATE!

# Canadian Open Science Conference
### October 27-29, 2026

<img src="https://carn-recar.ca/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/COSC_Bilingual_Logo_v1.1_20260302.webp" style="width:50%"><br>

The 2nd Canadian Open Science Conference will be held in Ottawa, Canada, from October 27–29, 2026. This conference, spearheaded and organized by PMC members Nico Alessandroni and Krista Byers-Heinlein, had its inaugural meeting in October 2025 and we are so excited it will have a second installment. Please visit the [conference website](https://carn-recar.ca/2nd-canadian-open-science-conference/) to save the date and join the mailing list. We will share additional details as they become available.
