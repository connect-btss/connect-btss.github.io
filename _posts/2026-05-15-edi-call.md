## 📢 CALL FOR ABSTRACTS: Advancing Justice, Equity, Diversity, and Inclusion (JEDI) in Big Team Social Science (Spanish, Portuguese, and English)

In collaboration with the [Avances en Psicología Latinoamericana (APL)](https://revistas.urosario.edu.co/index.php/apl) journal, The CONNECT Partnership has put up a call for papers for a special issue titled: **Advancing Justice, Equity, Diversity, and Inclusion (JEDI) in Big Team Social Science.**

APL is an open access journal edited by the Universidad del Rosario in Colombia that publishes articles in psychological research in Spanish, English, or Portuguese. The central aim of the special issue is to provide the first comprehensive examination of JEDI issues within BTSS, representing a core goal of the CONNECT Partnership: **to advance Big Team Science through increasing representation for currently under-represented regions in BTSS research.**

We are inviting various submission types in psychological research pertaining to JEDI in Big Team Science, covering topics such as governance, project management, authorship and credit sharing, networking, funding applications and management, language and time zone barriers to participation, epistemic decision-making, the value of time and other forms of invisible labor, Majority/Minority World researchers, race/ethnicity, gender, and disability, etc. **Submitted articles may be written in Spanish, Portuguese, or English.**

### ⏰ Abstract proposal deadline: July 31st, 2026
